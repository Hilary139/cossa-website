Brief History

The College of Sciences is one of the three pioneer Colleges at Afe Babalola University. It took off at the inception of academic work in January, 2010. The College has the colour ‘Gold’ as the College colour, and according to the University Colours, ‘Gold’ stands for ‘Wisdom’ The pioneer Provost College of Sciences is Professor Mike Olufemi Ajisafe: B.Sc (Hons); M.Ed; (Ohio); Ed.D (Temple), the first Professor of Sports Science in Africa.

Considering that the African continent is the worst hit by these challenges and the least prepared to face them, the College has taken a bold step to lead in providing the much needed intellectual basis for solving these problems. It is in the light of this herculean task that it was carefully developed from inception to this stage.

About Us

COSSA! Some see it as ‘College of Sciences Students Association’, others see us as an elite group of leaders in the college. Wherever you may find yourself, you’re 100% right. Although, take a step back, look at us as friends, brothers, sisters, in fact, as a family! We believe the students of this prestigious college thrive as ONE! The ultimate goal being - to improve the state of our wellbeing as Sciences students in academics,sports life,socially and in all other facets! There’s no place like home and we hope you feel at home in the College of Sciences. That is why we will continue to support you - the best colleagues and friends we could ask for. Love COSSA

Our Mission

To properly develop the students of the College of Sciences not just in the area of academics but in other facets such as; sports, socials, etc… so as to increase degree of value added by the association’s students wherever they may be found.

Our vision

The College of Sciences Students’ Association aspires to groom students who constantly make waves in the ever developing world of science and technology.
